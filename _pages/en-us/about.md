Chloé is a master's student in **Mental Health** at **McGill University**, co-supervised by **Dr. Ian Gold** and **Dr. Geneviève Sauvé**. A recipient of **3 excellence scholarships** during her first year, she holds a bachelor's degree in psychology from the same university and aspires to pursue doctoral studies in **clinical psychology**. 
<i class="fa-solid fa-brain" style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"></i>

Her research is part of the **Minds@Work-ADHD study**, a **randomized controlled trial** examining quality of work life among adults with ADHD. What particularly motivates her in her field is the opportunity to transform research knowledge into concrete interventions to improve the **quality of work life for adults with ADHD**. <i class="ti ti-mood-search" style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"></i>

In her free time, Chloé is an amateur athlete who enjoys **running** and **cycling**.
